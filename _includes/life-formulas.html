Life Formulas

ⓘ These are my notes to self that serve as reminder. Your perspective and frame of reference may differ from mine.


  • Pain + Reflection = Progress
  • Dreams + Failure + Perseverance = Prosperity
  • Patience + Righteousness + Courage => Success
  • Consistency >> Motivation & Direction >> Speed & Actions >> Words
  • Happiness = Wealth + Great Relationships + Gratitude
  • Great Relationships = Self-Love + Mutual : Love + Respect + Compassion + Understanding.
  • Social Media & News & Sugar & Alcohol ⇒ Cheap Dopamine = Lifestyle Pleasures to Avoid
  • Wealth = Health + Financial Capital + Time + Freedom.
  • Health = Balanced Diet + Exercise + Sleep
  • Sleep = No alarms + 8-9 hours + Circadian Rhythms
  • Exercise = High Intensity Resistance Training + Yoga & Meditation + Sports + Rest
  • Balanced Diet = Natural Whole Foods + Intermittent Fasting
  • Financial Capital = Income + Invested Savings * (Return on Investment)
  • Return on Investment = Buy-and-Hold Strategy + Market Valuation + Margin of Safety
  • Time in Market >> Timing the Market
  • Income = Accountability + Leverage + Specific Knowledge
  • Accountability = Extreme Ownership + Personal Branding + Risk Taking.
  • Leverage = Capital + People + Intellectual Property
  • Specific Knowledge = Having the ability to execute something that is not yet easily teachable by society.
