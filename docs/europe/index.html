Planning the Solo Trip of a Lifetime:

I have been wanted to see Europe for years, especially Switzerland, captivated by its stunning images in books, internet and on TV in the childhood. While I wasn't entirely certain which countries I wanted to visit, I had a few goals in mind:

  1. To be fully present‚Äîdetaching from the past and future‚Äîand truly embrace my own company.
  1. To try a complete digital media detox for a few weeks, having already significantly reduced my exposure to distractions like news, social media, and digital entertainment.
  1. To experience the challenges of navigating multiple countries alone with only limited possessions and time.
  1. To connect with new people‚Äîboth locals and fellow travelers‚Äîand gain fresh perspectives along the way.
  1. To give myself a genuine break from work, as I often neglect to take enough time off.

After some research and thought, I settled on visiting the United Kingdom, France, the Netherlands, Switzerland, and Italy.
